Backpackers Expedition

Explored from ages, there is will still be chances of you returning back for more. Packed with so much of traverse landscapes, cool and mild weathers, Pokhara has a kind of a magnetic aurora to it. Not just about the views and places to be, there are tons of things and activities that will have the adrenaline rushing in your veins. There are a number of epic things to do in Pokhara, especially for those chasing adrenalin, with white water rafting and some of the best paragliding on earth all available here.Somehow, despite the buildings getting taller and the crowds slightly busier in recent years, it still manages to retain an oasis-like feel; carefree and relaxed but with all the familiar traveller essentials. Although the place has somewhat adapted the aspects of modern western culture, you'll still be exploring the deep roots of peculiar Nepalese culture too. especially the Gurung culture.

Cultural Myriad

Stretched from Deurali, Salyan to the uppermost Lamjung, you'll discover the most of Indegenous cultures, their traditional living style and the places that never outgrow from.

Through the Annapurnas

Hiking in the imposing and impossibly beautiful mountains of the Annapurna Range is, without a doubt, the best thing to do in Pokhara and surrounds. We’ve done many multi-day hikes in Nepal, including the Annapurna Circuit, which most probably is the one most travelled. Things that tend to bring travellers to Pokhara are Mountain adventures and a chance to unwind. This has been la long know place of adventure and exploration for a large number of people. Along this, there certainly are multiple destinations that could be explored in and out through the city. Pokhara serves as a center for most of the popular places around. Manang, Mustang, Base Camps are among the places backpackers opt to explore. Some of the places that have been peoples favourite around Pokhara have been listed below.
